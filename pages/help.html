MY ACCOUNT
REGISTRATION AND LOGIN

New users need to create an account to shop or sell on the marketplace.

Common Issues:

  • Didn`t receive a verification email? → Check your spam folder or request a new one.
  • Phone number already in use? → Try logging in or resetting your password.
RESET PASSWORD

If you cannot login because you forgot your password, you can reset it easily.

Common Issues:

  • Didn`t receive a reset email? → Check spam/junk folders or try again.
  • Reset link expired? → Request a new one.
ORDERS AND SHIPMENT
ORDER TRACKING

Tracking your order helps you monitor its progress from purchase to delivery.

Common Issues:

  • Tracking number not working? → Try again after a few hours, tracking may take time to update.
  • No tracking number available? → Some orders only update when they are out for delivery.
SHIPPING STATUS

Your order may go through several shipping statuses. Here`s what they mean:

  • Order Confirmed - Your order has been received and is being processed.
  • Order Packed - The seller has packed your order and is preparing it for shipment.
  • Shipped - The package has been handed over to the courier and is on its way.
  • Delivered - Your package has arrived.

Common Issues:

  • Status hasn`t changed for a long time? → Some couriers take longer to update tracking. Contact support if it`s delayed beyond the estimated time.
  • Order shows `Delivered` but I haven`t received it? → Check with neighbours, security, or building reception. If still missing, contact customer support.
DELIVERY ISSUES

If there is a problem with your order`s delivery, here`s what you can do:

  • Package delayed beyond estimated delivery date? → Check the tracking status, contact the courier for more details, request assistance from customer support.
  • Package marked as `Delivered` but not received? → Check with neighbours or your building`s reception, contact the courier for proof of delivery, file a claim with the marketplace.
  • Received a damaged or incorrect item? → Take photos of the item as proof, contact the seller or marketplace support for a replacement or refund, check the Return & Refund Policy for eligibility.
PAYMENT AND REFUND
PAYMENT METHOD

Customers can choose from different payment options to complete their purchases.

  • E-Wallets - Dana, Shopee Pay, OVO, Gopay, etc.
  • Bank Transfer - Direct payment from a bank account.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) - Pay when the order is delivered (availability depends on location).

Common Issues:

  • Payment declined? → Check if your card has sufficient balance, verify your card details, or contact your bank.
  • E-wallet payment failed? → Ensure your account is linked and has sufficient funds.
REFUND PROCESS

Refund Eligibility:

  • Order was cancelled before shipment.
  • Item was damaged or defective upon arrival.
  • Wrong item received.
  • Package was not delivered within the promised timeframe.

Common Issues:

  • Haven`t received a refund? → Check the refund status in Orders or contact us.
  • Refund rejected? → Ensure you meet the return policy requirements and provided valid proof.
SHOPPING AND PRODUCTS
FINDING PRODUCTS

Customers can easily search for products using different methods.

Common Issues :

  • Can`t find a product? → Try using different keywords or check if the product is out of stock.
  • Wrong search results? → Use filters to narrow down the options.
LEAVING REVIEW

Leaving a review helps other buyers make informed decisions.

Review Guidelines:

  • Be honest and specific about the product`s quality.
  • Avoid offensive language or personal details.
  • Attach clear images if possible.

Common Issues:

  • Can`t submit a review? → Make sure the order is marked as delivered.
  • Review doesn`t appear? → Reviews may take time to be approved.
RETURN POLICIES

If a product is defective or incorrect, you may be eligible for a return. You need to give unboxing video as a proof, so you can return.

General Return Policy:

  • Eligible Items - Items must be unused, in original packaging, and returned within the allowed time.
  • Non-Returnable Items - Personalized, perishable, or digital products may not be eligible.
  • Return Timeframe - Returns are accepted within 1-7 days.

Common Issues:

  • Return request rejected? → Check if the item meets the return criteria.
  • Refund taking too long? → Check the refund processing time in the Payment & Refund section.
